What Affects Your Interest Rate?

📈

Credit Score

The biggest single factor. A score of 760+ typically qualifies for the best rates. Below 650 can mean rates 3–8% higher than advertised. Learn how to raise your score →

💰

Loan-to-Value Ratio

For mortgages and car loans, a larger down payment means lower LTV and lower rate. Putting 20%+ down on a home typically eliminates PMI and secures better rates.

📋

Debt-to-Income Ratio

Lenders want total monthly debt under 36–43% of gross income. Lower DTI signals lower risk and can unlock better rates. See all denial reasons →
