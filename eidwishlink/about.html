About Doratools

Spreading joy, one sacred greeting at a time 🌙

Who We Are

Doratools is a free online platform dedicated to helping Muslims and their loved ones celebrate special occasions with beautiful, personalized digital greetings. We believe technology should bring hearts closer together, especially during sacred moments like Eid.

Our tools are built with love, designed to be simple for everyone — young and old — and completely free to use with no registration required.

Our Mission

To make sharing Islamic greetings as easy, beautiful, and heartfelt as possible. Whether you're wishing family across the world Eid Mubarak or sending a personalized message to a friend, Doratools makes it memorable.

What We Offer

Currently our flagship tool is the Eid Wish Link Maker — generate a personalized animated Eid greeting link and share it on WhatsApp or Facebook. The receiver taps a lantern to reveal a beautiful sacred message with your name.

More tools are coming soon for other Islamic occasions including Ramadan, Hajj, and Milad un Nabi.
