Privacy Policy

We built DoraTools with privacy as a foundation, not an afterthought. Here is exactly what we collect, what we don't, and why — in plain English.

1. Plain-English Summary

🔒 The short version: DoraTools does not collect your name, email, IP address, or any personally identifiable information. All calculations run entirely inside your browser. No data is sent to any server we operate. The only exception is if you voluntarily submit our contact form — in that case, we receive only what you type.

2. Who We Are

DoraTools is a free online calculator platform hosted on GitHub Pages at https://doratools.github.io. We provide financial calculators and educational content at no cost and with no registration required. For privacy inquiries, use our contact page.

3. What We Collect

We collect only what is absolutely necessary to operate:

  • Contact form submissions: If you use our contact page, we receive your message and any email address you voluntarily provide. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.
  • GitHub Pages server logs: Our hosting provider (GitHub) may log standard server access data including IP addresses and HTTP request metadata. This is handled under GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not access, process, or retain these logs.

4. What We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifier (unless submitted via contact form)
  • Loan amounts, interest rates, or any financial inputs you enter into our calculators
  • Your location, device identifiers, or browsing history
  • Behavioral data, session recordings, or heatmaps
  • Any information about your income, credit, or financial situation

All calculator inputs and results exist only in your browser's local memory and disappear when you close or refresh the page.

5. Cookies & Tracking

DoraTools sets zero cookies. We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Plausible, Mixpanel, Hotjar, or similar)
  • Advertising or retargeting pixels (Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, etc.)
  • Session tracking, fingerprinting, or persistent local storage for user identification

Note on Google Fonts: Our pages load fonts from Google's servers. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's CDN, which may include your IP address. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We use non-blocking font loading to minimize this impact.

6. Third-Party Services

We use two third-party services for core infrastructure:

  • GitHub Pages (hosting) — GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Fonts (typography) — Google Privacy Policy

We do not use advertising networks, affiliate tracking services, or any third party that monetizes your personal data. We do not sell, rent, or share your information for commercial purposes.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because we collect virtually no personal data, most data rights are satisfied by default. However, your rights remain fully intact:

  • Right of access (GDPR Art. 15): We hold no personal data about you other than any contact form submission.
  • Right to erasure (GDPR Art. 17): Request deletion of your contact form message at any time via our contact page.
  • Right to opt out of sale (CCPA): We do not sell personal data.
  • Right to non-discrimination: Exercising any privacy right will never affect your access to our free tools.

8. Children's Privacy

DoraTools is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted information through our contact form, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this page periodically. Continued use of DoraTools following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or concerns: contact us here. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
