Our Approach to Cookies

DoraTools is built on a privacy-first philosophy. We use the absolute minimum cookies required for the Site to function. We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, social media trackers, or any third-party tracking technology of our own choosing.

✅ Short version: DoraTools does not track you. We do not store personal information in cookies. We do not share cookie data with advertisers or data brokers.

What Is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit. Cookies allow websites to remember your preferences and improve your experience. They cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

How to Control Cookies

All modern browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies. You can also set your browser to block cookies entirely. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect Site functionality. Here's how to manage cookies in major browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

LocalStorage (Not Cookies)

DoraTools may use browser LocalStorage to save your calculator preferences (such as your preferred currency) between visits. This data is stored entirely on your device, is never transmitted to our servers, and contains no personally identifiable information. You can clear LocalStorage through your browser's developer tools or site settings at any time.
