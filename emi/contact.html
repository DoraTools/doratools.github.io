Frequently Asked Contact Questions

How quickly do you respond?

Typically within 2 business days for general enquiries. Bug reports affecting calculator accuracy are reviewed within 24 hours.

Do you offer financial advice?

No. DoraTools provides informational calculators only. Our results are for estimation purposes — always consult a qualified financial advisor for personal financial decisions.

Can I use your calculators on my website?

Yes — contact us about our embed option. We're happy to work with publishers, financial blogs, and educational platforms.

Is DoraTools free forever?

Yes. All DoraTools calculators are completely free, with no signup required and no premium tier. We're funded by unobtrusive contextual advertising.
