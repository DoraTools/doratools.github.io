Our Story

About DoraTools

We build free, fast, privacy-first financial calculators — because everyone deserves clear answers about their money, without signing up or handing over their data.

Why DoraTools Exists

Every major financial decision — a 30-year mortgage, a car loan, a child's student debt — involves a number you can calculate in seconds. Yet most people either never run that number, or they find tools buried behind sign-up walls, cluttered with ads, or designed to funnel them toward a financial product.

DoraTools was built on a single premise: a free tool should actually be free. No account. No data sold. No "premium tier" that hides the amortization table. Just the calculator, the answer, and the knowledge to understand what it means.

What Makes Us Different

🔒
Privacy by Default

Zero personal data collected. No tracking cookies. No analytics. Everything you calculate lives only in your browser and is gone when you close the tab.

⚡
Speed as Respect

Every page targets Lighthouse 100 on mobile and desktop. A slow tool disrespects your time. Fast, instant, no loading spinners.

📖
Education Over Sales

We explain the formula, show the math, and provide real-world context. The goal is financial literacy — not selling you a loan or product.

🌍
Global by Design

Multi-currency support. No region-locked assumptions. Our tools work for anyone making financial decisions anywhere in the world.

♿
Accessible to All

WCAG AA accessibility standards on every page. Keyboard navigation, screen reader support, and sufficient color contrast throughout.

🔓
No Paywalls, Ever

Full amortization schedule, prepayment calculator, all 18 guides — completely free. We do not have a "Pro" tier. We never will.
